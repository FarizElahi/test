Your Questions, Answered

Find quick answers to the most common questions about our services and process.

Do you work with specific contractors or builders?

Yes, we have a trusted network of professionals, but we're also happy to collaborate with your preferred team.

Is there a minimum project size or budget?

We work on projects of all sizes, though we typically recommend a minimum budget to ensure quality results.

How do I get started with a project?

Simply reach out through our contact form—we'll schedule a consultation to discuss your needs and next steps.

Do you offer both interior and exterior design services?

Yes, we provide a full range of services—from interior styling to garden and landscape design.

How involved will I be in the design process?

We collaborate closely with you at every step to ensure the final result reflects your vision and needs.

What’s the typical project timeline?

Timelines vary based on project size, but most projects take between 4–12 weeks from start to finish.
